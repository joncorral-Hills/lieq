LieQ is a real-time digital party game where players speak confidently about topics they may or may not understand — and everyone else decides if they're telling the truth or spinning gold from thin air.

How It Plays — One Round in 5 Beats
🎤 Step 01
Speaker Chosen
One player is randomly selected as the Speaker each round via automatic rotation.
›
🎲 Step 02
Pick Stakes & Time
Speaker selects a topic tier (×1, ×2, ×3 points) and presentation length (60 or 120 sec).
›
🔮 Step 03
Jury Predicts
Before the Speaker starts talking, every juror secretly predicts Real or Fake for +15 bonus points.
›
🚨 Step 04
BS! Challenge
Any juror can tap BS! mid-speech. The room votes. A live Suspicion Meter on the TV shows the room's mood.
›
🏆 Step 05
Score & Reveal
Points awarded, prediction results revealed, personality badges earned. Leaderboard flips — repeat.
Signature Mechanics
✓
🔮 Secret Pre-Round PredictionEvery juror locks in a Real/Fake call before the Speaker begins. Zero dead time — everyone is mentally engaged from word one.
✓
📊 Live Suspicion Meter (TV)An anonymous needle on the shared screen shows the room's collective skepticism in real time. Comedy gold when it's pinned to FAKE and no one acts.
✓
🔥 Final 10-Second Hot ZoneThe last 10 seconds of every round apply a ×1.5 score multiplier to all BS calls. Tension spikes every single round.
✓
🎭 Personality Badges at Game EndSix badges (The Oracle, Sharpshooter, Ice Cold, Trigger Happy, etc.) drive post-game conversation and replay motivation.
✓
🔊 Procedural Sound DesignWeb Audio engine — no files, instant response. BS buzzer, challenge stings, Hot Zone alert, winner fanfare all synthesized in-browser.
